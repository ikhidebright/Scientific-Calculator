User guide

Mode

Tap the mode button to switch between Radians and Degrees for trigonomic functions. Deg or Rad in the top left corner of the display screen tells you what mode you’re in.

Shift

The shift button is used to switch functions (of the cos, sin, log, tan, e, and √) keys.

Example To get the sin⁻¹ of a number:
  1. Enter the number
  2. Press the shift button
    3. (sin changes to sin⁻¹)
  3. Click the sin⁻¹ button
Note: ** means raise to power ^. So don't be scared of seeing ** when you click ^.
